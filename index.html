SERVICES

Groundwater Assessment and Field Investigations

We strive to provide on-site groundwater investigations to determine the availability, behaviour, and sustainability of groundwater resources. This service includes:

i) Hydro-census surveys
ii) Borehole siting and drilling supervision
iii) Pumping test design, execution, and interpretation
iv) Groundwater monitoring
v) Field-based data collection and reporting

These investigations support informed decision-making for water supply development and regulatory compliance.

Who this is for:

i) Farmers and agricultural developments
ii) Property developers and estates
iii) Municipalities and water service providers
iv) Engineering and environmental consultancies
v) Industrial and commercial water users

Hydrogeological Modelling and Data Analysis

We develop conceptual and numerical groundwater models to understand groundwater flow systems and assess the impact of abstraction and climate change. This service includes:

i) Conceptual model development
ii) Numerical groundwater flow modelling
iii) Spatial analysis and mapping (GIS)
iv) Recharge estimation
v) Water balance assessments

Our modelling supports long-term groundwater planning, risk assessments, and sustainable resource management.

Who this is for:

i) Environmental consultants
ii) Engineering firms
iii) Mining and infrastructure projects
iv) Water resource planners
v) Regulatory and compliance-driven projects

Hydrochemistry and Water Quality Analysis

We assess water quality to determine its suitability for use and to understand groundwater processes. This service includes:

i) Interpretation of groundwater chemistry results
ii) Water quality classification and compliance assessments
iii)Hydrochemical diagrams and analysis
iv) Support for water use licence and environmental reporting

These assessments help clients evaluate risks to water quality and ensure regulatory compliance.

Who this is for:

i) Borehole owners and water users
ii) Agricultural and irrigation projects
iii) Industrial and commercial developments
iv) Environmental practitioners
v) Water use licence applications

Environmental and Regulatory Support

We provide professional hydrogeological input for environmental and water-use regulatory processes. This service includes:

i) Groundwater compliance monitoring and reporting
ii) Specialist hydrogeological inputs for Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA)
iii) Risk assessments for groundwater-dependent projects
iv) Water Use Licence (WUL) technical support
v) Ongoing technical advisory services

Our work aligns with South African regulatory requirements and industry best practice.

Who this is for:

i) Environmental assessment practitioners
ii) Developers and infrastructure projects
iii) Municipalities and public sector projects
iv) Consulting engineers
v) Clients requiring regulatory approval for compliance support
